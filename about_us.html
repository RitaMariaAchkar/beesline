Our Story

A personal journey that started in 1993

It started when our founders' mother had a harsh reaction to a luxury skincare product.

The two siblings — a Chemist and a Pharmacologist — discovered that most beauty products were full of harmful chemicals.

They turned to nature and the incredible benefits of bee byproducts, building formulas based on the science of apitherapy.

Two years later, after the success with dermatologists and their patients, Beesline was born.
